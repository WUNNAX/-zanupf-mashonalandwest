Vision 2030 Alignment

Zimbabwe’s Vision 2030 aims to create an upper-middle-income economy through digital transformation, innovation, and youth empowerment. This project trains unemployed youth in IT and tech while modernizing provincial digital infrastructure in Mashonaland West.

  • Web & App Development for e-Government and digital services
  • Cybersecurity & Database Management for secure data
  • AI & Machine Learning for smart decision-making
  • Digital Marketing & Communication to reach youth
  • Entrepreneurship & Fintech to drive innovation
